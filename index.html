GET TO KNOW ME

Hi I'm Mohak, born in Ratlam; did my primary schooling in Betul; moved to Bhopal in 6th standard, where I joined SPS and completed in 2015. Following my elder brother's path, I enrolled myself in the then best coaching -Vision Infinity. I was never serious about until it struck to me how important it is to excel in basic studies for having a good carrier and this turned over my life for good. I finally understood the role of working hard and being smart simultaneously. Being from the generation which saw digital transformation at its peak, I was always fascinated by automation and robotics. Starting from making battery-run boat and cars, to finally getting the opportunity to execute my erratic ideas in means of academic and internship projects, it's been an overwhelming journey for me. From being the mischievous and outspoken kid of my school batch to becoming a calm & introvert guy, I have learnt a lot. From my childhood I was technically inclined, I dreamt of becoming a pilot one day or atleast be in aerospace field; ended up taking Electrical branch; but then never worked in a single technical club of my institute; instead, got engaged with the social service body for 3 years. Let's just say noting went as I would have wanted. Do I have regret? Absolutely not. With all that off-track things happening, one thing became very clear to me, that life is all about exploring new things, enriching your experience, implementing ideas, learning from mistakes and standing back after a failure, but hey, taking a break is always fine!. I strongly believe in "a simple act of kindness creates an endless ripple". I wish to use my knowledge for something that matter.
